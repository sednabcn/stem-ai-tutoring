Venue & Safety

• Risk‑assess the space; ensure clear exits and first‑aid info.

• Suitable seating, lighting, and accessibility.

• Keep an incident log and report hazards to [Insert Role].

Materials & Readiness

• Bring printed resources or ensure device availability.

• Have a short no‑tech backup plan.

• Marking tools, timers, and mini whiteboards ready.

Conduct & Safeguarding

• Maintain professional boundaries; avoid 1:1 in closed spaces.

• Record late arrivals, incidents, and concerns the same day.

• Follow local photo/recording policy.
