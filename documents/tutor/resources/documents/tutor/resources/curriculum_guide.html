Overview

This template outlines the spine of a curriculum: intent, sequencing, delivery, and impact.

It is meant to be adapted by subject leads and tutors for different levels or cohorts.

Curriculum Intent

• Purpose: What knowledge, skills, and behaviours should learners secure by term end?

• Equity: How will the plan support diverse learners and close gaps?

• Employability / progression: Map to next steps or qualifications.

Learning Outcomes & Success Criteria

For each unit, state outcomes in learner‑friendly language and list success criteria.

Example:

Outcome: “Explain and apply Pythagoras’ theorem in 2D contexts.”

Success criteria: identifies right‑angle; substitutes correctly; computes; checks reasonableness.

Assessment & Evidence

Use mixed evidence: low‑stakes quizzes, exit tickets, practical tasks, and extended responses.

Template:

- Outcome assessed: __________________

- Method: Quiz / Project / Oral / Practical / Other

- Evidence stored: LMS / Drive folder / Paper

- Feedback cycle: Same day / Weekly / Fortnightly
