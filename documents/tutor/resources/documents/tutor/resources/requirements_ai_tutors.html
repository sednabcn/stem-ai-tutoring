Ethics & Transparency

• Inform learners when AI is used; explain limitations.

• Prohibit generation of harmful or biased content; verify outputs.

• Keep human in the loop for assessment and safeguarding decisions.

Data Protection

• Do not paste personal data into AI tools unless DPIA allows it.

• Use anonymised or synthetic examples where possible.

• Retain learning data per policy; enable deletion on request.
