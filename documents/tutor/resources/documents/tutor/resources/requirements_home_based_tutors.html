Workspace Standards

• Quiet, tidy area; desk‑height seating; camera angle at eye level.

• Stable internet and adequate power; backup plan for outages.

• Keep learning materials organised and labelled.

Safeguarding & Visitors

• Ensure a parent/carer is aware of session times and location.

• No unrelated visitors in the teaching area; follow lone‑working guidance.

• Follow photo/recording policy strictly.

Professional Boundaries

• Use official channels for communication.

• Keep personal areas out of camera view.

• Log sessions and outcomes in the LMS the same day.
