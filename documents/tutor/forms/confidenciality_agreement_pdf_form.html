I. Agreement Overview
This Confidentiality Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between the undersigned tutor ("Tutor") and the tutoring service organization ("Organization") to establish the terms and conditions regarding the protection and non-disclosure of confidential information encountered during the provision of tutoring services.
This Agreement applies to all forms of tutoring services including, but not limited to: online tutoring, face-to-face tutoring, home-based tutoring, and company-based tutoring arrangements.

III. Tutor Obligations and Responsibilities
  • Non-Disclosure: Tutor agrees not to disclose any confidential information to any third party without prior written consent from the student, parent/guardian, or organization.
  • Use Limitation: Confidential information shall be used solely for the purpose of providing tutoring services and shall not be used for personal gain or competitive advantage.
  • Data Protection: Tutor will implement appropriate safeguards to protect confidential information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure, including secure storage of physical and electronic materials.
  • Communication Security: All communications containing confidential information must be conducted through secure channels approved by the organization.
  • Material Return: Upon termination of tutoring services, Tutor agrees to return or securely destroy all confidential materials, documents, and information.
  • Social Media Policy: Tutor shall not post, share, or reference any confidential information on social media platforms or public forums.
  • Record Keeping: Any records maintained by the Tutor must be kept confidential and secured in accordance with privacy laws and organizational policies.
