Commission Rates: Applied to gross tutoring session fees before taxes

Package Upgrades: Changes take effect immediately, no refund for unused portion

Minimum Payout: $25.00 threshold for payment processing

Currency: All fees quoted in USD; international transactions may have additional costs

🔄 Refund & Cancellation Policy

  • Monthly packages can be cancelled at any time
  • No refunds for partial months of service
  • Downgrade takes effect at next billing cycle
  • Student session fees are non-refundable after service delivery
  • Dispute resolution available through platform support
  • 30-day notice recommended for package changes
