Company Overview

SiMLeng is a pioneering startup offering specialized math tutoring and AI consultancy services. Our mission is to provide customized learning in Data Science, Statistics, Python, C++, and Linux, for students and professionals at all levels. In addition, we help businesses navigate AI challenges through expert consultancy, especially with the LLM-HypatiaX project, which delivers AI-driven solutions and applications.

We are focused on helping individuals achieve mastery in technical fields and assisting organizations in harnessing the power of artificial intelligence to revolutionize their operations.

Our Achievements

SiMLeng has built a reputation for delivering effective solutions that help students grasp complex subjects and businesses incorporate AI technologies. We've made a meaningful impact by reducing operational inefficiencies, delivering personalized tutoring results, and integrating AI tools that drive growth.

Global Reach

Though based in London, UK, SiMLeng serves clients worldwide, from students seeking personalized tutoring to businesses looking for advanced AI consultancy. Our expertise spans across global industries, enabling us to provide customized solutions tailored to the needs of each region.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide information to website owners, and enhance your browsing experience.

Cookies contain information that is transferred to your device's hard drive. They help us recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several important reasons:

  • Essential functionality: Some cookies are essential for our website to function properly
  • Performance improvement: We analyze how visitors use our site to improve performance
  • Personalization: We remember your preferences to provide a better experience
  • Security: Cookies help us keep your account and data secure

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Purpose: Website functionality and security

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Retention: Session or up to 1 year

Analytics Cookies

Purpose: Understanding website performance

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often. This helps us improve our website's performance and user experience.

Retention: Up to 2 years

Marketing Cookies

Purpose: Relevant advertising and marketing

These cookies track your browsing habits to enable us to show advertising which is more likely to be of interest to you. They may also be used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement.

Retention: Up to 2 years

Functional Cookies

Purpose: Enhanced features and personalization

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced features. They may also be used to provide services you have asked for such as watching a video or commenting on a blog.

Retention: Up to 1 year

Your Cookie Choices

You have full control over cookies on our website.
